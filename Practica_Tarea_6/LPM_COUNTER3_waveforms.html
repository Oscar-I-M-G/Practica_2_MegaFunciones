Sample behavioral waveforms for design file "LPM_COUNTER3.vhd"

The following waveforms show the behavior of lpm_counter megafunction for the chosen set of parameters in design "LPM_COUNTER3.vhd". The design "LPM_COUNTER3.vhd" is a 4 bit dynamically controlled up/down modulus 6 counter. The counter supports an asynchronous load of data input. The counter has an asynchronous clear input.

Fig. 1 : Wave showing counter operation.

The behavior of the cin input is identical to the behavior of the cnt_en input. The output port cout will be asserted at the completion of count sequence. The ports cin and cout are used to chain multiple counters together.
